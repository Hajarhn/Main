flagship

Codebook Editor & Viewer

2026 · Post-COVID Network NL

A codebook tool researchers actually use

A browser-based system for editing and publishing the codebook of a national research network. The editor handles 500+ variables across 20 questionnaires with instrument matching, family-aware bulk updates, cross-row propagation, and copyright filtering. The public viewer lets researchers browse, search, and export — no install, no login, no patient data exposed.
