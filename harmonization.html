Harmonization & FAIR

Making health data interoperable

Health studies collect the same things under different names, in different languages, with different codes. My work is reconciling that — turning separate codebooks into shared, machine-readable meaning so data can be found, understood, and reused. This is the centre of what I do, and the strongest thing I bring to a team.
